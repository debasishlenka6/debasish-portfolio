Unifi Access Points & DHCP Configuration

Seamless wireless connectivity and network management across enterprise environments

Project Overview

Deployed and configured Unifi wireless access points with integrated DHCP services, ensuring seamless wireless connectivity and centralized network management across multiple enterprise environments with optimal coverage and performance.

Problems Solved

  • Poor wireless coverage and dead zones in office areas
  • Inconsistent DHCP IP address allocation
  • Lack of centralized wireless network management
  • Frequent wireless connectivity drops and performance issues
  • Inadequate guest network isolation and security

Implementation Steps

Step 1: Site Survey

Conducted wireless site survey to determine optimal AP placement and coverage areas

Step 2: Unifi Controller Setup

Installed and configured Unifi Network Controller for centralized management

Step 3: Access Point Deployment

Deployed Unifi APs with proper mounting, cabling, and power configuration

Step 4: DHCP Configuration

Configured DHCP scopes, reservations, and network segmentation policies

Step 5: Performance Optimization

Optimized channel allocation, power levels, and bandwidth management
