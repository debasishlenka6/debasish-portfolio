Technical Support & Troubleshooting

L1 & L2 support achieving 95% first-call resolution using advanced ticketing systems

Project Overview

Provided comprehensive L1 & L2 technical support services achieving exceptional 95% first-call resolution rate through efficient use of ServiceNow, JIRA, and Zoho Desk ticketing systems for enterprise environment.

Problems Solved

  • High ticket escalation rates due to inefficient troubleshooting
  • Poor customer satisfaction from delayed issue resolution
  • Lack of standardized support procedures
  • Inadequate knowledge base and documentation
  • Inefficient ticket routing and prioritization

Implementation Steps

Step 1: Process Analysis

Analyzed existing support processes and identified bottlenecks in ticket resolution

Step 2: Ticketing System Setup

Configured ServiceNow, JIRA, and Zoho Desk with proper workflows and SLA rules

Step 3: Knowledge Base Creation

Developed comprehensive troubleshooting guides and solution documentation

Step 4: Team Training

Trained support team on advanced troubleshooting techniques and tools

Step 5: Performance Monitoring

Implemented metrics tracking and continuous improvement processes
